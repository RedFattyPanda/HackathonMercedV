Tips on Common Issues you Might Encounter

Clogged Toilet Issues:
  1. Find a plunger.
  2. Place rubber end in toilet hole.
  3. Push and pull until unclogged.

Fire Alarm Issues:
  1. Know where your fire
    alarms are.
  2. Know how to turn off
    your fire alarms.
  3. Make sure they work by
    using their test button.
  4. Open the windows if it gets
    smokey.
  5. Buy a fire extinguisher.

Utilities Systems Issues:
  1. Know where your water
    heater is located and check
    if it is on.
  2. Check to see if all water
    taps are closed correctly
  3. Check to see if your
    stovetop works appropriately.

Bug Infestation Issues:
  1. Find out where the bugs
    are coming from.
  2. Get some bug spray.
  3. Spray the area where the
    bugs are coming from.
  4. If the issue is not resolved,
    then contact your landlord
    to hire an exterminator.
